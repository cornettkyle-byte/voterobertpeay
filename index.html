About Robert

Rooted in District 4

Robert Peay is a lifelong resident of the 4th District, a small business supporter, and a proud husband and father. His commitment is grounded in listening to neighbors and delivering practical solutions that keep the community safe, connected, and prosperous.

As your county commissioner, Robert has built coalitions across the district to deliver improvements without raising taxes. He believes leadership means showing up, doing the work, and staying accountable.
